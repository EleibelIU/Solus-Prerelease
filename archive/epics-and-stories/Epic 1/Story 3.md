### Fixed
Race
	This is determined at character creation
Racial Bonuses
	These are determined by selected race
		This should include things like any sort of dark vision, movement bonus
Ability scores
	These are determined at character creation
Skill Modifiers
	These are determined at character creation
Mastery
	This is determined at the appropriate level
Base Movement Speed
	This is determined by Race
Background
	 This is chosen at character creation
Character Name
	 This is chosen at character creation
Max Health Pool
Max Mana Pool
Character Size
	Determined by race

### Changeable
Skills
	These can be gained and changed in between the sessions, with a GM if playing with one for confirmation and clarity
Mastery Skills
	While the Mastery itself is not changeable the Skills within are same as normal ones.
Armor Damage Resistance (DR)
	The Damage Resistance (DR) of Armor will change based on the type of Armor worn
Weapons
	These can be changed at will so long as they are within the characters inventory
Level
	 This changes as you gain XP
Spells
	 You create your own so they can change at any time
Money
	 Can increase or decrease depending on how much you have
General Resources
	 These can be bought or used and you have a finite amount
Equipment
	 Tools, accessories and the like
Languages
	 Learned 

### Temporary
Modified Movement Speed
	Spells and items can effect this for a short time
Status Effects
	Status effects such as poisoned, encumbered, frozen, stunned and similar can be applied by spells, items, and other effects
Current Health
	Can be augmented by spells and similar effects
Curses
	Based on duration 
Environmental Effects
Current Mana
	Regenerates over time

### Long Term
Lingering Injuries
	Needs long periods of time to heal or proper treatment
Curses
	Needs to be removed over long periods of time or by other outside means such as removal by magic
Madness
Reputation

### Player-Controlled

- Skills

- Spells
- Money
- Resources
- Languages

### DM/System-Controlled

- Status effects
- Environmental effects
- Lingering injuries
- Long-term curses
- Madness
- Reputation changes


Character Joe

During intense combat Joe decides to cast a spell to temporarily give himself more HP than he normally has, and continues to fight. After the session Joe's player Bobby wants to swap a skill that increases his sneaking for one that can improve his buff his movement. Bobby tells Dan the DM about these changes he wants to make and Dan agrees since it's between sessions and they both mark down the changes in their notes and character sheet.
