Core Gameplay Loop.

World presents a problem-Players act based on problem-Consequences-Rewards-Adjust skills-Repeat 

### World presents a problem
The world or DM will present a problem, be it conversational, combative, puzzle solving, or timed event

### Players act based on problem 
Based on the problem presented Players will chose how to act based on everything available to their characters such as Stats, Skills, Equipment, Magic and such

### Consequences
Once the Players have chosen how to act, the system or DM then presents the Consequences for those actions good or bad.

### Rewards
Once the consequences have been resolved the rewards are given out, such as XP, Money, Weapons, and other loot

### Adjust Skills
In between sessions players may adjust skills by replacing them, increasing them or simply leaving them alone and making no change

### Repeat
The beginning of the next session will now start with the "world presents a problem" and continue from there
