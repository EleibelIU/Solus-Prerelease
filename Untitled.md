For MVP of Product (A system that is play-testable) , Epic 1 and Epic 2 are the requirements.

# Epic 1: Core Gameplay Loop

## Stories

1. Determine your mission statement which will justify your core gameplay loop

2. Define what constitutes a character - List out the systems that **influence** this Action resolution loop AKA what modifies it. Stats?

3. Define Character Modification - Can this sheet be altered after the fact?

4. Define what constitutes an NPC

5. Create the Core Gameplay loop / Action Resolution Loop

6. Determine Character Advancement

# Epic 2: Playtesting Standards

1. Create review process of all content in Epic 1
    
2. Run a scene/rough playtest of Epic 1 content
    

# Epic 3: Combat and Character Death

1. What is the state of the world as presented to the players? (Map? Grid)
2. Define Moving through the world state
3. Define protection from systems of Conflict
4. Define Conflict and its Violent Resolution
