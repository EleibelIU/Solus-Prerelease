## Retroactive Issues (touch whole manuscript)

These appear repeatedly and affect multiple chapters, examples, glossary, and reference tables.

1. ✅ **Secondary attribute is NOT permanent, NOT chosen at creation** — chosen per roll, per situation. Affects: Core Mechanics, Character Creation (Steps 7, 9), Attributes and Skills, example scene, skill table, glossary, reference entries.
2. ✅ **Initiative modifier is not permanently locked** — player chooses Body or Magic freely each combat. Affects: Character Creation (Step 5), Combat, example scene, glossary.
3. ✅ **Sanity rework** — Bloodborne Insight model. Low Sanity = perceiving hidden layers of the world and interacting with things others can't see. Only stat that can change during play. Fear belongs under Mind, not Sanity. "Eldritch horrors" is too narrow. Affects: Attributes and Skills, glossary.
4. ✅ **"Never say no" philosophy** — system enables, doesn't restrict. Remove prescriptive language that tells players what they can't do or funnels them into playstyles. Affects: weapon descriptions, discipline restrictions, background descriptions, Step 6 (disciplines).
5. ✅ **Mana is shared resource** — martial skills also cost mana (at lower costs), not just spells. Martial's 30 mana pool is not a limitation on their core loop. Affects: Character Creation Step 4, background descriptions.
