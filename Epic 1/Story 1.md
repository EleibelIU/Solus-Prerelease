Solus is heroic gritty realism. You can become legendary. The system will make you prove it. Players define their characters through skills, magic, and weapons they choose. Optimize or build to concept—the world tests both equally. When you ask 'can I do this?'--the system answers, not the GM. Shared mechanics for spells, weapons, and influence mean the rules work the same whether you're a player or running the table. Don't have a GM? The system doesn't care. The mechanics run the table with or without one. You will grow powerful. The world will not get safer. Every victory is earned through risk, and the threats never stop scaling with you.
