Character advancement works based on increasing Skills
You gain XP by progressing through and interacting with the world in exploration, combat, and conversation.
XP is earned in set listed amounts. Each Enemy Type (Minion-Boss) will have a set amount of XP it awards. 
Along with this Exploration encounters will similarly have XP that is gained by engaging with it.
Social/ Conversation encounters will also give XP based on success and failure.
In this you should be constantly gaining XP but Skills will cost progressively more as they increase.
The XP you gain is used to buy Skills and advance them. 
Each Skill has an initial price to acquire it, and within it will have several thresholds (Rank 1 - 10).
Once you hit the Skills threshold it can advance to the next stage where the process starts all over again.
Costs increase as you increase a Skills Rank, this cost is exponential.
Acquiring a new Skill will Start that Skill at Rank 1 and will need to progress through the ranks like normal.
Every other rank, starting at 2, will open another Support Skill Slot to a max of 5. 
All Rank 1 Skills start with 1 Support Skill Slot.
As you increase a Skills Rank you open the available Support Skill Slots that you can add too and augment the Skill with.
After gaining certain amounts of total XP you will unlock Mastery Skills and have a set amount of points to spend on them.
There are a finite amount of Mastery points that are less than the total number of Mastery Skills
The Max Support Skill amount is 5 per Skill
You can have a Max of 10 Skills active at any time but can acquire as many as you want so long as you have the XP to spend

XP is spent after each session.
